Here's a list of helpful commands for making plots.

/createplot {title} {x_axis_right_label} {x_axis_left_label} {y_axis_top_label} {y_axis_bottom_label} {min_x_value} {max_x_value} {min_y_value} {max_y_value}
/plotme {plot_id} {x} {y}
/removeme {plot_id}
/showplot {plot_id}
/listplots

Note that all the arguments for /createplot are optional. Here are a couple of /createplot examples:

/createplot -t This is a test! -xr Right -xl Left -yt A Top in My Dabney House? -yb No suprise, a Bottom -mx -10 -Mx 10 -my -10 -My 10

OR

/createplot --title This is a test! --xright Right --xleft Left --ytop A Top in My Dabney House? --ybottom No suprise, a Bottom --minx -10 --maxx 10 --miny -10 --maxy 10
